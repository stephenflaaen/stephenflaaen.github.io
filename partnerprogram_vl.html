Valley Leadership

About Valley Leadership

We’ve been strengthening Arizona leaders for over 45 years. How we prepare leaders is guided by our Principles of Doing – to lead most effectively in Arizona. And, no matter how you engage with us, you’ll come away with new skills and experiences, new connections, broader perspective and a personal plan for how you’ll make a difference going forward.


Valley Leadership's Programming

Catalyze

Catalyze sets up Arizonans to take action in the community. If you’re ready to make a difference or grow your impact, start your VL journey with Catalyze. With a format that includes virtual learning sessions and in-person events, you’ll be able to engage when and where it works best for you.

Explore

Explore takes people out of their community – and their comfort zone – to get to know the great diversity of the state and how our most pressing issues show up in rural, urban, suburban and Native communities. Participants will expand their perspective, knowledge of the issues and have the time and space to forge strong relationships and build connections in all corners of the state.

Impact Maker

Life-long leadership fueling long-term impact on Arizona's most pressing issues.

Civic Shift

Empowering the next generation of civic leaders through technology and community engagement.
