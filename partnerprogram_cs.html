Carnegie Young Leaders for Civic Preparedness Fellowship

Hosted by Institute for Citizens and Scholars

About the Program

The Carnegie Young Leaders for Civic Preparedness Fellowship is an immersive experience designed to empower the next generation of civic leaders. Participants will engage in workshops, hear from inspiring speakers, and collaborate on a real-world community project. This program provides a platform to develop critical thinking, public speaking, and project management skills. About Institute for Citizens and Scholars

The Institute for Citizens and Scholars is a non-profit dedicated to providing young adults with the resources and opportunities they need to make a positive impact in their communities. Our mission is to foster a sense of civic responsibility and equip youth with the tools for lifelong engagement.
